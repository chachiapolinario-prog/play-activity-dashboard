Risks and Blockers
High  Insufficient pipeline coverage
Current pipeline does not cover the $1.36M gap at a healthy 3x multiplier. Burst outputs must include a named list of net-new accounts to target in Q3 alongside structured outbound plays starting week 1 of the quarter.
Medium  High dependency on a few key accounts
If top 2-3 deals slip, the quarter is at significant risk. Mitigation: formalise close plans and exec sponsorship for big rocks before end of Q2; diversify pipeline with partner-sourced opportunities from the Burst.
Medium  Resource constraints
SE bandwidth is shared across the team. Demo and technical support for micro-events and partner labs needs to be locked in advance. Confirm SE availability (Toby, Zac, Dawn) for Q3 events at the Burst and agree on pod model and coverage expectations.
Low  Partner readiness on Plus positioning
Newer partners in the ecosystem still default to Grow or Advanced regardless of merchant revenue profile. Partner education on Plus as a growth vehicle is in progress via Wati but needs to accelerate before Q3 outbound plays begin.
